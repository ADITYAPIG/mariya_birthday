Our First Selfie

The very first photo the two of them ever took together — the frame where it all quietly began.

02

Our First Date

Their first ever date was a movie night out to watch "Bhoot Bangla 2." Somewhere in the dark of that theatre, she reached for his hand for the very first time — and he doesn't think he's ever felt happier than in that moment.

03

The Surprise Pickup

The second time she was making her way back to Kolkata after a long gap, he couldn't stand the thought of waiting even a little longer to see her. So that morning, he caught a train to the stop before hers, just to surprise her early and travel the rest of the way in together.

04

An Emotional Night

Their third date, another movie — this time "Chand Mera Dil." Somewhere through the film she leaned her head on his shoulder, held his hand tight, and drifted off comfortably right there beside him. It was the first time anyone had felt so at ease around him, and it quietly brought him to tears.

05

Our First Couple Photo

Just an ordinary day of wandering around together — and somewhere in it, they ended up with their first photo that finally looked like a real couple 🤭

06

One Month Together

The day they completed their first month together. The earrings she's wearing were his gift to her, and the two of them spent the whole day simply enjoying being with each other.

07

She Treated Me This Time

A light, fun date full of laughter — and for once, she was the one who picked up the bill 😁

08

When Rain Changed Our Plans

They'd planned a quiet sunset by the sea, but the rain had other ideas, and she was upset it fell through. So they improvised instead — a bike ride followed by a live jamming session at the mall. Somehow, the backup plan turned out to be just as amazing.

09

Between Exams, We Made Time

Their most recent date, squeezed in somewhere between exam schedules — a movie, "Dhamal 4." He'd been hoping for "Cocktail 2" instead, but as always, she had the final say 😑
